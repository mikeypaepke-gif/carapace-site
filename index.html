Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Live Vision

Point your camera at anything. Your AI sees it in real time. Read labels, analyze documents, identify parts, interpret X-rays — whatever's in front of you.

Voice Mode

Full hands-free voice interface. Talk naturally while driving, cooking, or working. Vision hits Gemini Flash first — fast and cheap — and only escalates to your full agent when it needs to.

Live Agent Monitoring

Watch your agents think in real-time. Monitor token usage, subagent trees, active sessions. Kill runaway processes with a tap.

Projects & Cron Jobs

Track workstreams, completion percentages, and scheduled jobs in one view. See what's running in the background without opening a terminal.

Zero-Trust Security

Encrypted end-to-end. No cloud relay, no data leaves your mesh. Pair via QR code, connect over Tailscale or direct TLS. Your conversations never touch a third-party server.

Multi-Gateway Control

Run gateways on your Mac, a VPS, and a Pi. Switch between them with one tap. Saved profiles, zero reconfiguration. One app controls your entire mesh.
