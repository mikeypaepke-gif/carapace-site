Install CARAPACE on Mac.
Three steps. ~5 minutes.

Download. Run the wizard. Scan a QR. The wizard installs everything for you — Tailscale, HTTPS, dependencies. You sit back and answer a couple of dialogs.

Before you start

  • A Mac running macOS Ventura (13) or later
  • Your Mac admin password (typed once)
  • An iPhone with the CARAPACE app installed
  • A login for Tailscale: Google, Microsoft, GitHub, or Apple ID
  • Optional — a free Gemini API key for vision mode (you can add it later)

You don't need a CARAPACE account. There's no email signup, no password, no telemetry. The Mac app is free forever — locked into our Terms.

1

Download CARAPACE

Get the DMG, drag Carapace.app to Applications, and double-click to open.

Download for
macOS — v1.9.24

2

Run the setup wizard

First launch starts a guided wizard. It walks you through everything — you'll click through a few dialogs and type your Mac password once.

What the wizard handles for you

  • →Tailscale — installs and signs you in if you don't have it. (Tailscale is a free private network so your Mac and iPhone can talk securely from anywhere.)
  • →HTTPS — flips on HTTPS for your tailnet and provisions a real cert for your Mac. iOS requires this.
  • →Dependencies — Homebrew, Node, the OpenClaw gateway. This is the only spot you'll type your Mac password.
  • →Gemini API key (optional) — paste one for vision mode, or skip and add it later from Settings → API Keys.
Already have Tailscale set up? Even faster — the wizard skips the Tailscale install and just provisions the cert. Make sure Tailscale on your Mac and on your iPhone are signed in to the same account.

3

Scan the QR with your iPhone

The wizard ends on a QR code. Open the CARAPACE iPhone app, tap Scan QR, point at it — done. (Need it again later? Menu-bar pearl → Settings → Show QR Code.)

You're paired.

The menu-bar pearl is your CARAPACE control center. Tap it on your iPhone and ask it something.
