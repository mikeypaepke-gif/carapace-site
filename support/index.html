Support

Stuck on something? Get a human, or jump into the full troubleshooting guide.

Reminder. CARAPACE is the iPhone client for your own self-hosted OpenClaw bridge. We don't operate a cloud, so most issues you'll hit are between your phone, your bridge, and your network. The contact paths below all go to a real human.

Get a human

Email
feedback@carapace.info
Bug reports, questions, feedback. Response within 48 hours on weekdays. Include your iPhone model + iOS version + bridge platform (Mac / Linux / VPS) for fastest help.
GitHub Issues — iOS app
github.com/mikeypaepke-gif/carapace-ios/issues
Public bug tracker for the iPhone app. Search existing issues first; open a new one if your problem isn't there.
GitHub Issues — Mac, Linux installer, marketing site
github.com/mikeypaepke-gif/carapace-site/issues
For bridge install scripts, the macOS Bridge app, or anything on carapace.info.

Refunds

In-app purchases are processed by Apple — we don't have access to the transaction and can't refund directly. Use Apple's standard process: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Report a Problem, or visit reportaproblem.apple.com. Family Sharing propagation is handled by Apple too.

Privacy questions

See the Privacy Policy for what data is sent where (short version: nothing touches our servers). Privacy-specific concerns or data-deletion requests: email feedback@carapace.info.
